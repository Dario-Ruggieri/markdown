# Text styling

You can modify your text like you want:

- ** This is bold text **
- - This is italic text \*
- ~~ This is mistake text ~~
- ** Bold and nested _italic_ **
- **_ All bold and italic _**
- <sub>This is subscript text</sub>
- <sup>This is superscript text</sup>
